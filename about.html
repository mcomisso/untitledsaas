About Forj Intel

Helping YouTube creators grow with data-driven insights and expert coaching.

What we do

Forj Intel combines analytics with human expertise. The app surfaces what's working on your channel - which videos drive subscribers, where viewers drop off, what topics resonate. Then coaching sessions help you act on it with a real strategy, not just more data.

Why we built this

We're creators who understand the struggle of growing a channel. YouTube Studio gives you numbers, but numbers without context don't help. We built Forj Intel because we wanted the tool we wished existed: analytics that actually tell you what to do next, backed by people who've been there.
