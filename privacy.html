Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Forj Intel (forj.cloud), we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. We aim to be straightforward -- no hidden surprises.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you interact with our platform:

  • Email address -- provided when you join our waitlist or contact us.
  • Name -- provided through our contact form.
  • YouTube channel URL -- optionally provided through our contact form so we can better understand your needs.
  • Usage data -- standard analytics information such as pages visited, time on site, browser type, and referring pages. This data is collected automatically and helps us understand how visitors use our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Communicate with you about the Forj Intel platform, including launch updates and feature announcements.
  • Provide, operate, and improve our services once you have access.
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the product experience.

We will not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of third-party services to operate the site:

  • FormSubmit.co -- processes form submissions (waitlist signups and contact forms). Your email address and any information you submit through forms passes through their service.
  • Analytics -- we use standard web analytics to understand site traffic and user behavior. This data is aggregated and not used to identify individual users.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you joined the waitlist, we keep your information until the platform launches and you either create an account or ask to be removed. We may also retain certain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as resolving disputes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your personal data from our systems.
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@forj.cloud. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use basic analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site. These cookies collect anonymous, aggregated data and do not track you across other websites. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site functionality may be affected.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users via email. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, reach out to us at hello@forj.cloud.
